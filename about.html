01 — WORLD OVERVIEW

A MODERN
GLOBAL CONFLICT

The world of AEGIS is built around a modern global conflict scenario, combining real-world military structure with heightened cinematic intensity. It is not a sci-fi world, and not a historical setting — it is a modern tactical battlefield universe designed for competitive PvP and story-driven missions.

Each Operation represents a critical flashpoint in an ever-evolving global conflict. Every map tells a story. Every firefight matters.

02 — LORE FOUNDATION

THE CONFLICT BEGINS

Rising worldwide tensions between covert military organizations and private armed groups

CHAPTER 01

THE FRACTURE

Global military alliances collapse. Private military companies rise from the ashes of dismantled state armies. Resources, technology, and influence become the new currency of war.

CHAPTER 02

FACTION EMERGENCE

Four dominant forces emerge from the chaos — each with their own ideology, resources, and combat doctrine. The VOX, BFS, ILF, and PRN begin their covert war for dominance.

CHAPTER 03

THE OPERATIONS

Critical flashpoints erupt across the globe. From frozen northern facilities to burning desert refineries — every Operation is a battle for control that shapes the world conflict.
